Bishop John Doe

Presiding Bishop

Bishop John Doe has faithfully served the body of Christ for over two decades. Called from a young age, his journey began in the heart of Kibera, where he witnessed firsthand the transformative power of the Gospel. Through seasons of challenge and triumph, Bishop John has remained steadfast in his commitment to shepherding God's people with wisdom, compassion, and unwavering faith.

Under his leadership, Diet of the Word International Church has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational ministry impacting lives across Nairobi and beyond. Bishop John is passionate about raising leaders, equipping the saints, and seeing the next generation empowered by the Holy Spirit.

Our Approach to Leadership

At Diet of the Word International Church, we believe in servant leadership—following Christ's example of humility, integrity, and love. Our leaders are committed to mentoring, equipping, and empowering every member to fulfill their God-given purpose. We value teamwork, accountability, and Spirit-led guidance in all we do.
