Adults Ministry

Equipping men and women to lead, serve, and grow in Christ (Ages 26+)

Growing Together in Christ

The Adults Ministry empowers mature believers to grow deeper in faith, serve in leadership, raise godly families, and thrive spiritually and socially. Through teachings, fellowships, couples' sessions, and service, we build a strong body of Christ.

Marriage Enrichment

Biblical sessions that strengthen couples, promote unity, and encourage Christ-centered homes.

Men & Women Fellowships

Monthly gatherings for encouragement, prayer, and building strong Christian friendships.

Career & Faith Talks

Workshops and discussions on integrating faith with work, business, and professional life.

Leadership in Ministry

Equipping adults to serve as leaders, mentors, and role models in the church and community.

Parenting & Family Life

Support and resources for raising children and building strong, faith-filled families.

Elder Mentorship Circles

Wisdom and guidance from seasoned believers for all stages of adult life.

Weekly Programs & Meetings

Adults ministry programs run weekly and monthly, offering spiritual growth opportunities across age and life stages.

  • Sunday Discipleship: 9:00 AM – 10:30 AM (before service)
  • Monthly Men & Women Fellowships: 2nd Sunday of each month
  • Couples' Devotion Night: Last Friday of the month
