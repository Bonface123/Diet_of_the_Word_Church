Youth Ministry

Equipping the next generation to lead, love, and serve (Ages 19–25)

Empowering Young Adults for Christ

The Youth Ministry empowers young adults to live for Christ boldly in today's world. Through Bible studies, mentorship, outreach programs, and fellowship events, we cultivate purpose, purity, and leadership in every young believer.

Bible Study & Prayer

Weekly gatherings to study God's Word and pray together for real-life impact.

Mentorship & Coaching

Personal and group mentoring to help youth navigate purpose, faith, and decisions.

Outreach & Evangelism

Serving the community and sharing Christ's love through missions and outreach.

Worship & Fellowship

Dynamic worship nights and social events to build faith and friendships.

Leadership Development

Workshops and opportunities to grow as servant leaders in church and society.

Faith & Career Talks

Guidance for balancing faith, studies, and career as a young Christian adult.

Weekly Schedule & Structure

Our youth programs run weekly with additional mission opportunities, university outreaches, and youth conferences.

  • Youth Bible Study: Wednesdays at 6:00 PM
  • Sunday Youth Forum: 11:30 AM after main service
  • Monthly Outreach Missions: Last Saturday of the month
