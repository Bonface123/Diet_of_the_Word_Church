Toddlers Ministry

Raising little hearts in the love of Christ (Ages 1–6)

A Joyful Start in Faith

Our Toddlers Ministry provides a safe, nurturing environment for your little ones to experience the love of God through songs, play, stories, and foundational Bible truths. Guided by trained caregivers and Sunday school teachers, our toddlers learn through joy and interaction.

Bible Songs

We teach toddlers joyful songs that help them remember God's love.

Story Time

Bible stories come alive with pictures, puppets, and interactive lessons.

Creative Play

Hands-on crafts and playtime encourage learning and friendship.

Sunday School Corner

A special space for learning about Jesus in age-appropriate ways.

Snack & Rest

Healthy snacks and quiet time help little ones recharge and feel at home.

Caring Teachers

Our team is passionate about nurturing faith and joy in every child.

Service Times & Age Information

Toddlers Ministry runs during all main Sunday services. Children aged 1–6 years are welcome. Parents must sign in and out for safety.

  • Drop-off starts: 9:15 AM
  • Pick-up ends: 12:30 PM
  • Clean, child-safe classrooms with soft floors and age-appropriate materials
