Teen Ministry

Raising young warriors for Christ (Ages 13–18)

Empowering Teens for Life & Faith

Our Teen Ministry provides a supportive space for teenagers to grow in Christ, ask hard questions, and develop leadership and character through discipleship, peer groups, and relevant Bible-based teaching.

Bible & Life Talks

Relatable messages to guide teens through identity, purpose, and real-world challenges.

Identity in Christ

Helping teens discover their God-given identity and value in a confusing world.

Leadership Workshops

Workshops and activities to develop confidence, teamwork, and servant leadership.

Events & Camps

Retreats, camps, and fun events that build faith, friendships, and memories.

Peer Prayer Groups

Small groups for prayer, support, and accountability among teens.

Purpose Discovery

Guiding teens to find and pursue their God-given calling and dreams.

Weekly Sessions & Gatherings

We meet every Sunday and on select Fridays for deeper engagement. All teenagers are welcome!

  • Sunday Sessions: 10:00 AM – 12:30 PM (during service)
  • Friday Fellowships: 5:30 PM – 7:30 PM (biweekly)
  • Location: Teen Ministry Hall (Annex Building)
