Sunday School Ministry

Training children in the Word of God (Ages 6–13)

Building Faith, One Child at a Time

Our Sunday School Ministry is a cornerstone of the church's mission to raise godly children. With lessons rooted in Scripture, creative activities, and caring teachers, we disciple children in their early faith journeys.

Bible Teaching

Each class uses age-appropriate curriculum to help kids know God's Word.

Memory Verses

Children memorize and recite Scripture, hiding God's Word in their hearts.

Worship Songs

Joyful singing and praise help children express their love for Jesus.

Faith-Based Crafts

Hands-on projects reinforce Bible lessons and encourage creativity.

Interactive Games

Games and activities make learning about faith fun and memorable.

Christian Etiquette

We teach kindness, respect, and Christ-like character in every lesson.

Class Times & Structure

Sunday School classes run during all Sunday services. We group children into classes by age for focused, fun learning experiences.

  • Lower class: 6–9 years
  • Upper class: 10–13 years
  • Service time: 9:30 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Parents sign in and out before/after class
